Quick and easy, scrambled eggs are high in protein! They make a great breakfast or addition to any othe meal.

  • 4 Large eggs
  • 1/4 Cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Whip the eggs and cream in a bowl until soft and fluffy.
  2. Preheat pan or skillet to medium heat.
  3. Gently pour eggs onto cooking surface.
  4. Cook until just starting to bubble and then flip and fold into themselves.
  5. Remove from heat while firm and slightly 'wet' looking, allow a couple minutes to cool for moisture to evaporate.
