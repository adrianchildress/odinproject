Tiramisu, Italian for 'cheer me up', is a delicious coffee and cream based dessert.

  • 4 Large egg yolks
  • 1/2 Cup sugar
  • 3/4 Cup heavy cream
  • 1 Cup mascarpone
  • 1 and 3/4 Cups espresso
  • 2 Tablespoons cognac
  • 2 Tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Approximately 24 ladyfingers
  • 2 Ounces of bittersweet chocolate shavings
  1. Whip the egg yolks until they are soft and fluffy.
  2. Continue whipping the eggs as you add the sugar and cream.
  3. Mix in the marscapone and cognac.
  4. Dip the ladyfingers into the espresso and set into a pan.
  5. Mix the remaining espresso into the egg mixture.
  6. Layer the egg mixture onto the ladyfingers.
  7. Top with cocoa powder and chocolate shavings.
  8. Place into a refridgerator for 6 hours to set.
