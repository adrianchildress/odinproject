Delicious chocolate chip pumpkin cookies. The texture is not quite the same as standard chocolate chip cookies. However, there is a delightful tangy taste and enjoyable cake-like texture.

  • 1/2 Cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 Cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 Cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 and 1/2 Cups flour
  • 1/4 Teaspoon coarse salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 Teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 Cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  1. Start by softening the butter and preheating the over to 350 F.
  2. Combine the butter, sugar, vanilla extract in a large bowl.
  3. Continue to mix and combine in the pumpkin puree.
  4. Mix in the flour and rest of the ingredients.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.
